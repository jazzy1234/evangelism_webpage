Matthew 28:18-20

18And Jesus came and said to them, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. 19Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, 20teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age.”


The Mission

Our mission is simple. To spread the Gospel to the ends of the earth, starting in the DMV area.

  • Primary Objectives

    Bring the Good News to our community
    • Sin destroys lives but we will bring them the ultimate answer
    • Display the love of God by proclaiming the plan God enacted for our sin
  • Encourage/Equip believers to share the Gospel
    • Display the love of God by serving with believers from different churches and backgrounds
    • Encouraging other believers to step out of their confort zone and exercise their evangelistic muscles

  • Secondary Objectives

    Establish a foothold with the Gospel
    • Make connections with other believers in the area for prayer, empowerment and encouragement
    • Inspire a fire in the hearts of believers on campuses/communities that we evangelize
  • See people get saved/come to church
    • Planting seeds for the Holy Spirit to do His work in transforming hearts
    • Playing our part in bringing about genuine tansformation which makes genuine disciples
